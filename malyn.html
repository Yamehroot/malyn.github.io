My Autobiography

My name is Ma. Lyn O. Ompad and I was born and raised in beautiful island which is Olango Island .My father's name is Marcelino A. Ompad and he work as a tricycle driver. Mirasol O. Ompad is my mother's name and she is a housewife. We are six siblings, three boys and three girls. I am rank three in our family, our oldest is our brother followed by our sister and me.

After completing my senior high, I enrolled in Lapu- Lapu City College . It was a privilege to be a student in LLCC, I joined an organization such as LCW or League of Computer Wizard which is an organization for only the computer technology department and I am a currently and the out-going secretary.

During my free time, I spend it in planting of vegetables in our small garden and also planting ornamental plants. And also I am a person that loves to wander around.

Looking back on my life so far, I am proud of My Achievements. I believe that "If my mind can conceive it,if my heart can believe it, then I can achieve it.".
